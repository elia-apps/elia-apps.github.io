Privacy Policy

Elia Feeding · Last updated 13 July 2026

Privacy is part of what Elia Feeding is, not just a box to tick. Feeding and diaper logs are sensitive family context, so the app is built to keep them on your device and out of anyone else's hands.

The short version

  • No account and no sign-in.
  • No data is collected by Elia.
  • Your data is never sold or shared.
  • Everything is stored locally on your device.
  • No cloud sync and no automatic backup of app data.

What the app stores

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Breastfeeding, bottle, and diaper entries you log.
  • Timing and history derived from them.
  • Your settings.

There is no backend, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Data leaving your device

Data leaves your device only when you choose to export or share it, through your device's own share options. You pick the destination. Elia does not upload your log to any service of its own.

Backups

Automatic cloud backup and device-to-device transfer of app data are disabled. Your feeding log moves only through an export you start yourself.

Network and permissions

Core logging does not require registration or a connection. The app avoids unnecessary permissions and contains no tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app's data, or uninstalling the app, removes the local data from your device. Because nothing is stored elsewhere, that is all it takes.

Children

Elia Feeding is a tool for parents and caregivers. It logs the care you record; it does not collect data about anyone from any other source.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at feeding@getelia.app.

Elia Feeding is a calm memory aid, not a medical tool. It does not give medical advice or define what is normal. For anything about your baby's health, talk to your pediatrician or midwife.
