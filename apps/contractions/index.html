Elia Contractions

A calm companion for birth.

A contraction companion for families during labor, built around one belief: during labor, the interface should reduce stress, not add to it.

Coming soon
What it is

Just enough, exactly when it matters.

Elia Contractions helps you

  • Record contractions with one clear tap.
  • Review timing and intervals at a glance.
  • Stay focused during a contraction.
  • Stay present between them.
  • Keep a simple, honest history.
  • Share a clear summary with your care team, if useful.

What it is not

  • A medical device or diagnostic tool.
  • A replacement for doctors or midwives.
  • A pregnancy or baby tracker.
  • A subscription trap.
  • An advertising surface.
  • Something that tells you what to do.
Why it exists

Born from a real birth.

Induction at 39+0. A balloon catheter in the morning. Propess in the afternoon. Within an hour, contractions felt nearly continuous, with only rare pauses.

The partner searched for a contraction app. The one they found lagged. The main button never made it clear whether tracking had started or ended. Useful features were locked behind a subscription. After three contractions, it told the family to go to the hospital — where they already were, under medical supervision.

The app added stress. Elia is the answer to that moment.

A son was born at 19:44, weighing 2760 g. Elia was born with him.

The promise

Calm, private, and yours.
