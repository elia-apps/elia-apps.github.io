Privacy Policy

Elia Contractions · Last updated 13 July 2026

Elia Contractions is built to be private by design. It has no account, no backend, and no tracking. This policy explains, in plain language, what that means.

The short version

  • No account and no sign-in.
  • No data is collected by Elia.
  • Your data is never sold or shared.
  • Everything is stored locally on your device.
  • Nothing is uploaded to any Elia server.

What the app stores

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Contraction start and end times.
  • Session history derived from those times.
  • Your settings.

The app does not store medical records, location, identity, baby data, or health-provider data.

Data leaving your device

Data leaves your device only when you choose to export or share it, using your device's own share options. You pick the destination. Elia does not upload your log to any service of its own.

Backups

Automatic cloud backup and device-to-device transfer of app data are disabled. Contraction data moves only through an export you start yourself.

Network and permissions

The app does not require an internet connection to record contractions, and it avoids unnecessary permissions. It contains no advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app's data, or uninstalling the app, removes the local data from your device. Because nothing is stored elsewhere, that is all it takes.

Children

Elia Contractions is intended for adults during pregnancy and labor. It does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at contractions@getelia.app.

Elia Contractions is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. Always follow the guidance of your doctors and midwives.
